Cockroach at the Go London User Group

22 Jul 2015

Here’s the recording of a talk I gave at the 15th London Go User Group about Cockroach DB. I’m particularly excited about all the work that’s being done on the SQL layer: In the mere 24 hours that have passed since the presentation, SQL support has already taken a couple of steps forward. It’s satisfying to see it all come together.

Check our the video below or take a look at the slides.

Thanks to the London gophers for having me. It’s only been my second time in London (the first time being a 7th grade weekend trip) and I’ve enjoyed it a lot.
